## :material-account-supervisor: EPIC-Led Handover

The **incoming EPIC** leads the handover process to ensure:

- **Complete overview** of the department
- **All patients progressing** appropriately  
- **No patients missed** or forgotten
- **Clear priorities** for the incoming shift
- **Situational awareness** across the team

!!! success "Handover Process"
    The incoming EPIC will systematically run through **every patient assigned to an emergency physician** to ensure comprehensive coverage of all active cases.

---

## :material-format-list-checks: What to Include

!!! warning "Clarify Handover Status"
    **Discuss all your patients** during handover. If you're leaving, make it clear which patients have outstanding tasks requiring action versus those already referred or progressing without further input needed.

When handing over your patients:

!!! example "Essential Information"
    
    **Clinical Summary**
    
    - Presenting complaint
    - Key findings and investigations
    - Working diagnosis
    
    **Current Status**
    
    - Where are they in their ED journey?
    - Results pending?
    - Awaiting specialty review?
    
    **Outstanding Tasks**
    
    - What needs to be done?
    - Any time-critical actions?
    - Expected disposition

---

## :material-alert: Priority Patients

!!! danger "Highlight These Patients"
    - **Deteriorating** or potentially unstable
    - **Time-critical tasks** due soon
    - **Complex social situations** affecting disposition
    - **Specialty referrals** pending response
    - **Outstanding critical results** expected
    - **Access block** preventing admission
    - **Safeguarding concerns**

---

## :material-lightbulb: Best Practice Tips

!!! tip "Effective Handover"
    - **Be on time** - Handover starts promptly
    - **Be prepared** - Review your patients beforehand
    - **Be concise** - Focus on key information
    - **Speak clearly** - Ensure everyone can hear
    - **Ask questions** - If anything is unclear
    - **Take notes** - Document key points for incoming shift
    - **Stay until complete** - Don't leave before handover finishes unless asked to go and tidy up tasks

---

## :material-information: After Handover

Once handover is complete:

- **Outgoing team** - Finish off tasks that can be completed and tidy up patients (usually have time left in shift after handover)
- **Incoming team** - Work in allocated areas and start seeing new patients unless otherwise directed
- **EPIC** - Retains departmental overview and control
