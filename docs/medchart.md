## :material-check-circle: Key Features

!!! tip "What MedChart Can Do"
    - Electronic prescribing of medications
    - View patient medication history
    - Check for drug interactions
    - Access formulary information
    - Document administration
    - Generate discharge prescriptions

---

## :material-alert: Important Reminders

!!! warning "Safe Prescribing"
    - Always verify patient identity before prescribing
    - Check allergies and intolerances in the system
    - Review current medications for interactions
    - Ensure correct dose, route, and frequency
    - Document clinical indication where required
    - Complete and approve prescriptions promptly
