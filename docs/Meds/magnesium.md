# Magnesium Calculator

> Quick converter for magnesium dosing. Convert between the common units used in clinical practice and calculate vials needed.

!!! info "Clinical Context"
    **MgSO₄ heptahydrate** (MgSO₄·7H₂O) is the form listed in medchart, though most clinical guidelines simply refer to "magnesium" or "MgSO₄".
    
    **Standard preparation**: 2 mmol/mL (10 mmol in 5 mL vial)

!!! note "Key Conversions"
    - **1 g MgSO₄·7H₂O** ≈ **4 mmol Mg²⁺**
    - **% w/v** = grams per 100 mL of solution
    - Always verify doses
