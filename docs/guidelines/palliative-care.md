## :material-file-send: Referral Process

!!! warning "Urgent Referrals"
    If the referral requires an **urgent HPCT review**, please call **7484** first before submitting the form.

### Referral Forms & Guidelines

[:octicons-link-external-24: Referral to MidCentral Specialist Palliative Care Services](https://midcentraldhb.sharepoint.com/sites/ControlledDocuments/ControlledDocumentsAll/Forms/AllDocs.aspx?id=/sites/ControlledDocuments/ControlledDocumentsAll/Referral%20to%20MidCentral%20Specialist%20Palliative%20Care%20Services%20INTERACTIVE%20-Form-.pdf&parent=/sites/ControlledDocuments/ControlledDocumentsAll){:target="_blank" .md-button}
[:octicons-link-external-24: Guidelines for Referral to HPCT](https://midcentraldhb.sharepoint.com/sites/CS-ClinicalServices/SiteAssets/SitePages/Palliative-Care/Guidelines-for-Referral-to-HPCT--1-.doc?web=1){:target="_blank" .md-button}

**Process:**

1. Download and complete the referral form
2. Email to: [HPCT@midcentraldhb.govt.nz](mailto:HPCT@midcentraldhb.govt.nz)

The referral form is used for:

- Inpatient assessment by the Hospital Palliative Care Team
- Community follow-up by Arohanui Hospice
- Admission to the Arohanui Hospice inpatient unit

**Please indicate the required service on the form.**

!!! note "Important"
    While the HPCT and Arohanui Hospice collaborate closely, they are separate entities. The HPCT does not determine acceptance to Arohanui Hospice services.

## :material-pill: Discharge Prescribing

[:octicons-link-external-24: Prescribing for Symptom Management at End of Life](https://midcentraldhb.sharepoint.com/sites/CS-ClinicalServices/SiteAssets/SitePages/Palliative-Care/2024-Prescribing-for-symptom-management-at-the-end-of-life.docx?web=1){:target="_blank" .md-button}
[:octicons-link-external-24: MedChart Prescribing Syringe Drivers (Inpatient & Discharge)](https://midcentraldhb.sharepoint.com/sites/CS-ClinicalServices/SiteAssets/SitePages/Palliative-Care/MedChart-Prescribing-Syringe-Drivers--Inpatient-AND-Discharge-.pdf?web=1){:target="_blank" .md-button}

A handy guide for appropriate prescribing of **subcutaneous PRN medication** for palliative patients being discharged home or to ARC (Aged Residential Care).

The guide shows:

- Dosages
- Strength and amounts of ampoules required
- Safe prescribing for end of life care

!!! tip "Complex Symptoms"
    If the patient has complex or uncontrolled symptoms, please contact:
    
    - **HPCT** on 7484 (during business hours)
    - **Arohanui Hospice** on [06 356-6606](tel:+6463566606) (after hours)
