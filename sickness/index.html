    Sick Leave Procedures

    Immediate Action Required

    Call as soon as you know you'll be unable to work. Early notification helps us arrange cover and maintain patient safety.

    How to Call in Sick

    • Step 1: Call Early

      Contact as soon as you know you'll be off

      The sooner we know, the better we can arrange cover

    • Step 2: Contact Numbers

      Call the appropriate contact based on shift timing

      See contact details below

    • Step 3: Documentation

      Follow up with any required paperwork

      As per hospital policy

    Contact Information

    Who to Call

    EPIC and MAU

    • Phone: See phone numbers

    EPIC then contact MAU on the next working day

    • Available 24/7
    • Phone: See phone numbers

    Important Guidelines

    Best Practices

    • Call, don't text - verbal confirmation is essential
    • Provide expected duration if known
    • Medical certificate may be required for extended absence
    • Return to work - confirm fitness before resuming duties

    Fit for Work

    You must be well enough to provide safe patient care. If in doubt about your fitness to work, discuss with your supervisor.
